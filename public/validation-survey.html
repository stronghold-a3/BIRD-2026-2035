Enablers are the physical and digital systems that make everything else possible: roads, power, internet, health, education, water.

Connectors link BARMM to outside markets: trade, exports, and the BIMP-EAGA regional corridor (Brunei-Indonesia-Malaysia-Philippines East ASEAN Growth Area).

Financiers provide capital in ways aligned with Islamic (Shariah) principles: Islamic Banking, Takaful (Islamic insurance), Waqf, and Microfinance.

The "asset paradox": BARMM has valuable land, resources, and people, but very little of this wealth is turned into usable investment capital.
